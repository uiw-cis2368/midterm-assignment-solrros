Early life:

I was born and raised in San Antonio , Texas. I am the oldest of 3 and grew up on the Southside of town , which primarly consisted of hispanics in poverty. Growing up , I realized that most people from my side of town did not care for school or felt that they weren't worthy of any type of higher education. Thankfully my mom , dad and stepdad did their absolute best to keep me on the right track and ensured I had goals and aspirations beyond grade school. From a very early age , I dreamed of having a life that would provide for my family and since then, I have been on a mission to make that dream a reality. I will always be proud of where I come from and will be forever grateful to my community , as it has made me who I am today.

Today:

Currently , I am in my senior year at the University of the Incarnate Word in San Antonio, where I study Criminal Justice and Computer Information System. The passion I have for both of these programs is indescribable , I love what I study. Of course , as a first-generation student my first couple years of undergrad were extremely hard to navigate and came with a lot of challeneges like imposter syndrome. Despite that, UIW has always made me feel welcomed and i'm glad that I attended a school that surrounds itself with positivity, kindness and community. Additionally , I have met some of the greatest people and mentors , who have constantly encouraged and guided me through the toughest of times.

Future Plans:

As my final year of undergrad comes to an end, it's a bittersweet moment but I can't help but be excited to futher my higher education. For the next remaining months of 2022 , I will be solely spending my free time applying for Criminal Justice Master and Doctoral programs that will help me continue to contribute research into the field. Hopefully this next stage in my life will bring clarity to my long-term career goals and aspirations. Although I plan to branch out to universities outside of San Antonio , I hope to stay in Texas. I have a lot of work ahead of me but I can't wait to graduate and use my free time before gradute school with family & friends. Wish me luck and thank you for reading!
